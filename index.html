About MarketPrice+

Fighting Price Gouging
Across Nigeria

MarketPrice+ is a collaborative initiative by the Federal Government of Nigeria to bring transparency, fairness, and accountability to market prices across the country. Whether you're buying food, electronics, or fuel — know the approved price before you pay.

✓ Real-time approved price database updated daily
✓ Verified vendor marketplace with compliance badges
